Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use QuickClean (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photo Library Access

To enable the photo cleaning and management features, we require access to your photo library. We only access photos when you actively use the App, and:

  • Photos are analyzed locally on your device
  • We do not upload your photos to any server
  • You can revoke photo access permission at any time through system settings

1.2 Contact Access

To provide contact management features, we require access to your contacts. We:

  • Only access contacts when you use the contact management feature
  • Do not upload your contacts to any server
  • All contact processing is performed locally on your device
  • You can revoke contact access permission at any time through system settings

1.3 Photo Metadata

When processing photos, the App may access photo metadata including:

  • Photo creation date and size
  • Image file format
  • Location information (which can be removed to protect your privacy)

1.4 App Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the App, including:

  • Number of App launches
  • Frequency of feature usage
  • Crash reports and performance data

This data is completely anonymous and does not contain any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide photo analysis, duplicate detection, and similar photo identification features
  • To compress photos and videos to save storage space
  • To manage and clean contacts
  • To remove photo location information to protect your privacy
  • To improve App performance and user experience
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your photos, contacts, or any personal data with any third parties. All processing is performed locally on your device.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • All photo and contact analysis are conducted on your device
  • We do not store any photos or contacts on our servers
  • All temporary data is automatically cleared when the App is closed
  • When you choose to compress photos or remove location information, the modified files are saved to your device's photo library

5. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke photo and contact access permissions through system settings
  • Delete the App to clear all related data
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.

7. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be announced within the App or on our website.
